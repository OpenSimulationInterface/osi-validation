Detailed Description

Definition of supplementary sign types.

Number Type name Requirements
0 TYPE_UNKNOWN Type of supplementary sign is unknown (must not be used in ground truth).
1 TYPE_OTHER Other (unspecified but known) type of supplementary sign.
2 TYPE_NO_SIGN There is no supplementary sign (must not be used in ground truth).
3 TYPE_VALID_FOR_DISTANCE Traffic sign is valid for a certain distance (given by TrafficSignValue::value in e.g. meter - TrafficSignValue::value_unit).
4 TYPE_VALID_IN_DISTANCE Distance to the start of validity of the traffic sign (given by TrafficSignValue::value in e.g. meter - TrafficSignValue::value_unit).
5 TYPE_TIME_RANGE Time range for validity of traffic sign (starting from first TrafficSignValue::value and ending with second TrafficSignValue::value, both defining e.g. time of day in minutes since midnight - first TrafficSignValue::value_unit and second TrafficSignValue::value_unit). Day of the week starting from third TrafficSignValue::value and ending with fourth TrafficSignValue::value with third TrafficSignValue::value_unit and fourth TrafficSignValue::value_unit).
6 TYPE_WEIGHT Restriction of the validity of the traffic sign depending on the vehicle's weight (more than TrafficSignValue::value e.g. in kilogram - TrafficSignValue::value_unit).
7 TYPE_RAIN Restriction of the validity of the traffic sign to wet / rainy conditions.
8 TYPE_FOG Restriction of the validity of the traffic sign to foggy conditions.
9 TYPE_SNOW Restriction of the validity of the traffic sign to snowy / icy conditions.
10 TYPE_SNOW_RAIN Combined restriction of the validity of the traffic sign to rainy or snowy conditions denoted by a single supplementary sign (instead of two signs of #TYPE_RAIN and #TYPE_SNOW respectively).
11 TYPE_LEFT_ARROW Direction, left-pointing.
12 TYPE_RIGHT_ARROW Direction, right-pointing.
13 TYPE_LEFT_BEND_ARROW Direction of potential danger, left-pointing.
14 TYPE_RIGHT_BEND_ARROW Direction of potential danger, right-pointing.
15 TYPE_TRUCK Valid for heavy trucks.
16 TYPE_TRACTORS_MAY_BE_PASSED Passing, only tractors allowed.
17 TYPE_HAZARDOUS Hazardous.
18 TYPE_TRAILER Valid for vehicle with trailers.
19 TYPE_NIGHT Valid at night.
20 TYPE_ZONE Zone.
21 TYPE_STOP_4_WAY Stop 4 way.
22 TYPE_MOTORCYCLE Valid for motorcycles.
23 TYPE_MOTORCYCLE_ALLOWED Only motorcycles allowed.
24 TYPE_CAR Valid for cars.
